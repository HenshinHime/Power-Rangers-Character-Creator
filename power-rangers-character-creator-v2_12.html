POWER RANGERS RPG

Character Creator

1 Character Concept

Begin by deciding who your character is. Consider their personality, backstory, and what motivates them. Define the core concept before making mechanical choices.

2 Choose Your Origin

Your Origin represents your core personality and background. It determines your starting Health, Ground Movement, Essence bonus options (+1 to one), bonus skills, languages, and a special Origin Benefit.

3 Choose Your Ranger Role

Your Role determines your Ranger color, Essence adjustments (automatic bonuses), Power capacity growth, Power regeneration rate, Armor Training, starting skills, and equipment.

4 Choose Your Influences

Influences are life experiences that shape your character. You get 1 free Influence with no Hang-Up. Additional Influences (up to 3 total) require accepting their Hang-Up. Each Influence grants an Influence Perk.

5 Allocate Essence Points

Distribute your 12 free Essence Points across the four attributes. Combined with your Origin (+1) and Role (+3) bonuses, your total should equal 16.

6 Allocate Skills

For each Essence category, you may spend skill points equal to that Essence Score. Each rank costs 1 point. Specializations also cost 1 point each.

7 Perks & Grid Powers

Select any perks granted by your Origin and available Grid Powers.

8 Configure Your Zord

Rangers gain access to their Zord at 3rd level. Configure your mighty machine by selecting its team type and features.

9 Level Up

Optionally advance your character to a higher level. Each level grants Essence increases, new skill ranks, and special abilities based on your Role.
